President Ice Melt Has a Message

 

Share

  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on X
  • Share on Bluesky
  • Share on LinkedIn
  • Share on SMS
  • Email this Page
 

Published:

Febuary 8, 2026
 

UNITED STATES -- People across the United States are furious at polar bears fleeing their melting homes. The Onion interviewed Mr. Peter Polarfobic on Wednesday, who fumed, “I remember twenty years ago when there were zero of them down here, but now, whichever way I look, I see them everywhere.” The Onion also spoke with the SnowBall Saviors. Their spokesperson told us the following: “We find it ridiculous that so many people are anti-polar bear. They simply want a place to experience the United States' beauty and warmth.”

 

Last week, The Onion received several reports: a pack of polar bears lounging at the beach in Florida. Even more worrisome, there was a report of two polar bears raiding a cooler. Luckily, no one was killed. This invasion was quickly noted by President Ice Melt after receiving hundreds of letters from his constituents.

 

He promptly made a speech targeted at the polar bears. “To the furred beasts invading the United States, I ask you to get out. Return to the puddle of your homeland and stay there. For if not, I will create the Interspecies Climate Enforcers (ICE) to ensure your deportation.” In response to this message, the SnowBall Saviors are outraged, calling it a heartless attack on innocent animals. 

 

Protests began nationwide, with people waving signs telling the bears to leave. Peter Polarfobic led one of these protests in Florida, shouting, “They’re stealing our sun chairs!” Meanwhile, The Onionfound a post on X from Beatrix the Bear, “These humans melt our home, then complain about new neighbors! What are we supposed to do? Move to the South Pole? This is outrageous.”

 

During this panic, we noticed a major increase in the profits of major companies. We interviewed one of the executives, “This crisis? It could not be better for business! People are scared of animals that would do us no harm. As long as the company's profits continue to rise, I’ll remain in my lounge chair and enjoy the chaos. Also, one of my scientist friends told me that the melting is due to human activity. However, I think it is crazy that we could have such an impact.”

 

In his most recent national address, President Ice Melt proclaimed once again that the United States is not for polar bears, stating that the Interspecies Climate Enforcers are working around the clock to move the polar bears back to their rapidly melting land, so Americans can enjoy their rising sea levels all by themselves.
